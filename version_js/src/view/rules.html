Having 2 ENSIIE made their ranking in durable development decrease because their were spending twice their usual consommation of energy. That's why you absolutely need to close the door !

What are the rules ?

You will have to spend energy (E) to buy machines while preventing your durable development (DD) from reaching 0 (meaning the destruction of your world).
Your goal will be to produce and send to the gate 10000 resources in order to close it. Beware ! Each resource send will produce a garbage that you will have to handle otherwise your DD will decrease by 1.

How should I play ?

At the start, you will have 2 sources that produces 3 resources/10 turns. You will have to buy a machine called a collector to mine resources from a source (or garbage from the door) then send them using either a conveyor belt or a cross belt to the gate.
You can send garbage to a recycling center or in a junkyard according to your strategy.

But in order to buy a machine, you will need E and DD ! These are produced by the student of the ENSIIE, called FISE (initial training course, 1E 1DD per turn) or FISA (part-time training course, 4E or 4 DD per 2 turns).

If you have enough E and DD, you can hire a Staff that can reduce the cost of a machine, hire a lot of students, ... Be sure to check out all of them in-game !
